# Stats Cards Design Variations for DevZone

## Design 1 - Current (White Cards)
- White background
- Blue icons
- Clean minimal style
- 5 cards in 2 rows (3+2)

## Design 2 - Dark Modern  
- Dark slate background
- Colored borders
- Hover effects
- Single row layout

## Design 3 - Gradient Cards
- Gradient backgrounds per card
- Bold colors
- Shadow effects
- Modern look

## Design 4 - Glassmorphism
- Frosted glass effect
- Transparent backgrounds
- Backdrop blur
- Premium feel
